ABOUT US :

FASHION BEATUY was launched in 2003. Dubbed as the Modern Arab Woman’s Source of Fashion our readers refer to FASHION BEATUY as their seasonal guide on “what to buy.” The Arabic & English language glossy focuses on the latest in fashion, beauty and luxury. Distributed on the newsstand and on a complimentary basis in 9 Arab countries, FASHION BEATUY reaches the most affluent women in key markets such as The United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar and Egypt.
“At FASHION BEATUY we actually have personal contacts with many of our readers , which gives us the opportunity and edge to target to their needs and desires, and stay atop through reader’s suggestions and comments”.
